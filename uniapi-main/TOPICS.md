## How to Add Topics

1. Go to https://github.com/LiuLucian/uniapi
2. Click the ⚙️ icon next to "About" section
3. In the "Topics" field, add the topics above (one at a time or comma-separated)
4. Click "Save changes"

## Why Topics Matter

Topics help users discover your project:
- Show up in GitHub topic searches (e.g., https://github.com/topics/social-media-api)
- Appear in "Related repositories" sections
- Improve SEO and discoverability
- Signal what your project does at a glance
